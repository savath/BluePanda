About Steak Shack Hawaii

Sharing good food and great memories


Steak Shack Hawaii is located on the Lovely island of Oahu, in downtown Waikiki. We come out with products that people love. Our original teriyaki sauce goes great with the steak and chicken plates! Every steak plate is crafted to fill the tummies of the people visiting and from Hawaii!

If you have any questions about us, our food, or just want to send us a thank you, feel free to contact us. Our goal is to exceed the expectations of our customers while providing you with a full tummy and a warm sense of Aloha!

"Only the best chicken and beef are used in each of our plates. We check the quality of our products to ensure that you are only getting the finest quality hawaiian style platters for the best price on the island."
